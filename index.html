Bread vs pasta/Noodles

The debate that will end all debates. Which is better? And which could you live without?!

Bread

Bread is not as malleable and adaptable as pasta/noodles, you can make a variety of dishes with them and they come in extremely unique shapes. But bread is that girl: she's stand alone and she doesn't need to be apart of a flashy dish. However, she does come in some variety as well. Let's look at four unique iterations of this iconic food:

  1. Bagel
  2. Naan/Pita/Flat Bread
  3. Croissant
  4. General Bread (slice it, eat it, etc)

Pasta

Pasta basta. Compared to pasta, bread is giving normcore. It's easy, classic, simple, yummy....but is it groundbreaking? If you go to a restaraunt are you ordering bread? No, that often comes for free or on the side...she's not the main attraction. But pasta, pasta she's the whole d*mn meal. Take a look at four different pasta/noodle dishes:

  1. Penne
  2. Macaroni
  3. Egg noodle
  4. Udon
